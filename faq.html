Frequently Asked Questions

What types of jobs do you take on?

Beginner to intermediate tasks like drywall repair, painting, appliance installs, babyproofing, smart home setups, and more.

Do you do emergency repairs?

We currently focus on scheduled work. Contact us with your situation and we’ll see if we can help.

What should I prepare before a service visit?

Please clear the workspace, secure pets, and let us know if there are any access concerns in advance.

How do I pay for services?

We accept e-transfer and cash. Receipts available upon request.
